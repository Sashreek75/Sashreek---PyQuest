## üéÆ Key Features

### 1. Interactive Quests

Content is organized into modules and immersive Quests that transform lessons into challenges. Learners progress through levels while building real projects, not just completing exercises.

### 2. Virtual Coding Sandbox

A built-in sandbox lets users experiment freely with code, test ideas, and grow their skills without fear. This encourages creativity and deeper understanding.

### 3. AI-Evaluated Quizzes

PyQuest uses AI-driven assessments to evaluate solutions, provide feedback, and reinforce learning outcomes. Students gain confidence and clarity on their progress.

### 4. Gamified Progress Tracking

Profiles, dashboards, levels, achievements, and stats make learning visible and motivating. Progress tracking helps students stay committed to long-term goals.

### 5. Career Architect

One of PyQuest's most unique features. The Career Architect analyzes user interests and skill progress to generate a realistic, step-by-step roadmap toward careers in software engineering, data science, AI research, and other tech fields.
